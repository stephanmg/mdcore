This routine differs from \#runner\-\_\-dopair\-\_\-verlet in that instead of storing a Verlet table, the sorted particle ids are stored. This requires only (size\-\_\-i + size\-\_\-j) entries as opposed to size\-\_\-i$\ast$size\-\_\-j for the Verlet table, yet may be less efficient since particles within the skin along the cell-\/pair axis are inspected, as opposed to particles simply within the skin of each other.
